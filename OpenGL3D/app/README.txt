Instructions:
Arrows to move up/down/left/right.
W to move forward.
S to move back.
Y to yaw right.
H to yaw left.
R to roll right.
F to roll left.
P to pitch up.
L to pitch down.
C to switch camera.
1 to enable/disable fog.
2 to enable/disable dynamic positional light.
3 to enable/disable skybox.
4 to enable/disable particle system.
